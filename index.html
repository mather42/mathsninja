My Mission ✨

I want to share the excitement and creativity of Olympiad-style problem solving with as many young minds as possible. This site is primarily dedicated to guiding students through the “further investigations” questions on UKMT's extended solutions to their maths challenges, and championing Pólya’s four-step cycle — understand, plan, do, look back. I aim to show show that real progress comes not from chasing one correct answer, but from exploring ideas, testing conjectures, and building a versatile strategy toolkit.

Every write-up on this site models that process in clearly, so learners can confidently tackle new challenges. Ultimately, I hope to empower young mathematicians to meet complex problems with creativity, resilience, and a deep appreciation for the beauty of mathematical discovery.
